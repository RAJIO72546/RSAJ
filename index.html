About RSAJ

The **Roller Skating Association of Jharkhand (RSAJ)** is a non-profit organization that promotes roller skating in the state of Jharkhand, India. The association is dedicated to the development of roller skating as a sport and aims to provide a platform for skaters to showcase their skills and compete at various levels.

RSAJ is registered under the **Roller Skating Federation of India (RSFI)**, which is the governing body for roller skating in India ². The association has several district units and registered skaters who participate in district and state championships ². RSAJ has been actively organizing various events and competitions to promote roller skating in the state.

The association has a Facebook page ¹ and a Facebook group ³ where they share updates about their events and activities. RSAJ has been working towards creating awareness about roller skating and its benefits, such as improving physical fitness, balance, and coordination.

In addition to promoting roller skating, RSAJ has been working towards creating a safe and inclusive environment for skaters. The association has been organizing workshops and training sessions to educate skaters about safety measures and injury prevention ². RSAJ has also been working towards creating opportunities for skaters from underprivileged backgrounds to participate in roller skating ².

RSAJ has been successful in promoting roller skating in Jharkhand and has been instrumental in creating a platform for skaters to showcase their skills. The association has been working towards creating a safe and inclusive environment for skaters and has been organizing various events and workshops to promote roller skating in the state. If you are interested in roller skating, RSAJ is a great place to start!
